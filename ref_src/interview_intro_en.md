## Tips for Delivery

- **Pace:** Aim for ~150 words/minute. This script is ~800 words for the spoken parts, fitting within 5-6 minutes. Expand with examples if you have more time.
- **Tone:** Confident but not arrogant. Use "we" for team achievements, "I" for personal contributions.
- **Adapt:** If the interviewer seems engaged on a topic, go deeper there and skip less relevant parts.
- **Numbers matter:** Always mention specific metrics (62.5%, 500 games, 30 seconds, etc.) — they make your claims credible.
